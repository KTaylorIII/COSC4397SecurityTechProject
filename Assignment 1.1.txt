This mini assignment focuses on the initial stage of the HCD process, specifically on Inspiration.  In this assignment, you will work towards defining the problem for your chosen project topic.  In doing so, use the basic principles discussed in class to answer the following questions:

Who are your stakeholders?

What is the role of each stakeholder?

Who are the intended beneficiaries?

What are persona for each stakeholder?

What is your plan to understand the need?

Having done so, enumerate up to 20 questions you want to ask from your stakeholders.
